About Maramadakki

History & Map

Maramadakki is a vibrant village known for its fertile fields, timeless temples, and close-knit community. Generations of farmers and artisans have preserved traditional wisdom while embracing modern progress.

The village celebrates festivals together, protects its environment, and supports education for every child, making Maramadakki a model rural community.
